
# ⚖️ AI Legal Assistant

An AI-powered voice-based legal assistant that helps people understand their legal rights in simple language.

## 🚀 Features
- 🎤 Voice chatbot (Hindi + English)
- 🧠 AI legal guidance
- 📄 Legal notice PDF generator
- 🔍 Case understanding
- 📢 Voice response (text-to-speech)

## 🧑‍💻 Tech Stack
- Frontend: HTML, CSS, JS
- Backend: Node.js
- AI Logic: Custom + GPT Ready

## 🎯 Use Case
Designed for people who cannot afford lawyers and need quick legal guidance.
